My Story

My name is Maira Tokayeva. I am UI/UX designer with a background in ecology and environmental management. My background as an environmental specialist has taught me the importance of empathy and attention to detail, which I combine with my UI/UX design skills for design projects. During my studies and work in the field of environmental management, I acquired the skills of conducting research and analyzing data and statistical information. Now, I want to apply my knowledge and analitycal skills on UX/UI design, that combines both my passion for design and data research and analysis.
